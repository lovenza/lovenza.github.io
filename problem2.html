Data Wrangling

This R code transforms the instacart dataset, creating three data subsets for visualization: plot_1_data summarizes total sales by department, reordering them by volume; plot_2_data takes a 10,000-row random sample belonging only to the top 10 best-selling departments identified from the first summary; and plot_3_data filters for the ‘snacks’ department, then aggregates the total item count by the hour of the day.
